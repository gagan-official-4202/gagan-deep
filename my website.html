Collins Aerospace is a leading global aviation and defense technology company and a primary subsidiary of RTX Corporation. It provides intelligent solutions, including avionics, aircraft interiors, advanced structures, and life support systems, for commercial, military, and space applications.The company was formed in 2018 through the merger of Rockwell Collins and UTC Aerospace Systems. It serves the aerospace industry globally, with a robust footprint across the Americas, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region. For instance, Collins maintains a massive operations and engineering presence in India (with major facilities in cities like Bengaluru) and continues to expand its manufacturing and R&D footprint globally, such as investing in radar facility expansions in Largo, Florida.
